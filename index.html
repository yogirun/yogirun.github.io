Ask Question?

Queries on Yogi Run

Getting back to start point?
After the completion of the event, runners may avail the complimentary bus service from the base of the nandi hills. Bus will leave every 20 mins after 7;30 am.
do we have Aid Stations?
Of course! We have Aid Stations stocked with water, energy drinks, biscuits, bananas along the route. First Aid stations and medical outposts are also present along the route, in addition to ambulances. There will also be a recovery room at the finish point for any emergencies. Please refer to the route maps in the respective categories.
How is the route for the run ?
The route for the 10k run and Half Marathon is certainly challenging! With a combination of rolling hills amidst nature , we promise you “it’s a challenge worth every step”. A well planned training schedule containing sufficient hill training will go a long way. The 5K Run follow an easier and relatively flatter route,
Getting to the start point ?
We have arranged for the KSTDC bus , Bus will leave the kanteerava stadium at 4:00 AM. You can contact the following person for assistance in getting into the bus - DHIRAJ 89048 90519
Can spectators attend the post-race Finish event?
Absolutely !
Is The Yogi Run timed??
Yes it is a timed event. Finisher get a medal and a certificate
What does the Registration fee Cover ??
The Registration fee covers the following: Goodie Bag An official race BIB (RFID Tagged with the exception of the 5K Fun Run which is not a timed run) Water, energy drinks and refreshments along the course. Medical Support Post-race refreshments Finisher’s medal Timing Certificate (Soft Copy with the exception of the 5K Fun Run which is not a timed run) To ensure that the timing is recorded, the Runner must cross all the timing mats provided at various locations. The Timing Certificate (Soft Copy) will be provided for runners who have completed the Full Marathon / Half Marathon/ 10K Run in under 6:30 hrs / 3:30 hours / 2 hours respectively. The 5K Fun Run is not a timed event and therefore will no timing certificate will be provided.
Are there refunds?
We do not have a refund policy. Once a registration is confirmed the registration fee is non-refundable. The registration is also non-transferable. If an event is held as advertised, we cannot issue refunds. The logistics of setting up and planning the event make it impossible. Just make sure you are good to go before signing up.
